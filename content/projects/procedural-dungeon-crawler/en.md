A high-performance 3D first-person dungeon crawler built with modern web technologies. Explore procedurally generated dungeons, battle physics-driven enemies, and descend deeper into the abyss.

!Game Screenshot

- Procedural Dungeon Generation: Every run is unique with randomly generated layouts, rooms, and enemy placements. - Physics-Based Combat: Real-time combat system using @react-three/rapier for satisfying collisions and projectiles. - Dynamic Enemy AI: Encounter both melee and ranged enemies with distinct AI behaviors. - First-Person Experience: Smooth 3D controls with mouse-lock and responsive movement. - Progression System: Navigate through multiple floors with increasing difficulty. -
