A structured mission-based role-playing game set within a sprawling 3D high school campus. Experience a detailed simulation of student life combined with a phased objective system that drives a deep, evolving narrative.

Lost Campus RPG is an exploration-heavy experience where players balance daily academic life with complex mission chains. Built on a robust phased progression architecture, the game challenges players to complete specific objectives, unlock new campus areas, and interact with a dynamic cast of characters.
