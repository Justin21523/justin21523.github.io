A high-speed, Sonic-inspired 3D infinite runner built with React, Three.js, and Zustand. Experience fast-paced highway adventure with advanced movement mechanics, procedural level generation, and a classic ring-based health system.

- Advanced Movement: Jump, Double Jump, Dash, Slide, Wall Jump, and Roll for fluid traversal. - Infinite Highway: Procedurally generated road chunks and backgrounds for an endless experience. - Sonic-Style Health: Collect rings to protect yourself.
